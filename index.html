Empowering Incarcerated Individuals Through Labor

What We Do

Liberty Labor Solutions offers a wide range of job opportunities within the correctional system, allowing inmates to build critical life and trade skills that prepare them for a successful reentry into society.

Our Past

Since 1957, Liberty Labor Solutions has been at the forefront of recreational prison labor. We began as a non-profit, but slowly expanded our boundries into what it is today. Now we find ourselves in countless prisons all across the country.

Programs We Offer

The Green Initative

We save the planet one step at a time, inmates will grow organic crops, sort recycling, or plant trees outside for recreational purposes all in an attempt to make our world more green.
